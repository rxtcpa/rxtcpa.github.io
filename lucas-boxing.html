🛡️ WARRIOR DEFENCE 🛡️
⏱️ 5 MINUTES
We don't train TO hit. We train so we NEVER have to.
But if someone attacks — extreme violence of action. ⚔️
⭐ THE WARRIOR SPIN ⭐
1. Someone pushes you from behind
2. STOMP your front foot (base!)
3. SPIN around to face them
4. Hands UP in guard position
5. LOUD VOICE: "STOP!" 🗣️
6. Front kick (TEEP) if they come closer
Practice 10 times every session. Both sides.

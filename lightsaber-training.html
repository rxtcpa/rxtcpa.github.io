THE 7 FORMS OF LIGHTSABER COMBAT
💡
Tap any Form to open it. Start with Form I — even Yoda began here. Master each one before the next.
I
FORM I
SHII-CHO
The Way of the Sarlacc
▼
🟡 BEGINNER — START HERE
The oldest and most basic form. Every single Jedi — even the greatest Masters — began with Shii-Cho. It teaches you to strike in all seven directions and move your feet the right way. Without this, nothing else works.
⚡ KEY MOVE
The Wide Sweep — a big horizontal swing that can knock a weapon from someone's hand. Reach matters more than speed here.
🏋️ DRILLS TO PRACTISE
Strike all 7 zones: head, left shoulder, right shoulder, left hip, right hip, left knee, right knee
Footwork squares — step forward, back, left, right while keeping blade up
Wide sweep practice — slow at first, then full speed
Two-handed grip power strike into a hanging target
⚔️ MASTERS OF THIS FORM
Yoda Luke Skywalker All Jedi Younglings
II
FORM II
MAKASHI
The Way of the Ysalamiri
▼
🔵 DUELIST — PRECISION FORM
This form is about being smart, not strong. Tiny movements, perfect timing. You dodge by millimetres instead of metres. A Makashi fighter never wastes a single step. It's the art of the perfect duel.
⚡ KEY MOVE
The Makashi Riposte — you deflect the enemy's blade with the smallest possible movement, then instantly counter-strike before they recover.
🏋️ DRILLS TO PRACTISE
One-handed grip practice — control with your wrist, not your whole arm
Small deflections — practice redirecting a slow-swing with minimal movement
Footwork lunges — one big step forward, extend blade, then back
Hold a "ready pose" for 30 seconds — perfect stillness
⚔️ MASTERS OF THIS FORM
Count Dooku Asajj Ventress
III
FORM III
SORESU
The Way of the Mynock
▼
🩵 DEFENDER — THE UNBEATABLE WALL
Soresu is the art of survival. Stay calm, make yourself small, deflect everything. You don't attack — you wait until the enemy makes a mistake. The best Soresu fighters have NEVER been beaten. Patience is the weapon.
⚡ KEY MOVE
The Deflection Wall — blade in constant motion in front of your body, redirecting every attack. Think of spinning a shield faster than blaster bolts can travel.
🏋️ DRILLS TO PRACTISE
Figure-8 blade movement — keep the blade spinning in a figure-8 in front of you for 60 seconds
Make yourself small — bring elbows in, feet together, guard up
Breathe drill — match 4 slow deflections to one full breath
Hold defensive stance for 2 minutes without moving feet
⚔️ MASTERS OF THIS FORM
Obi-Wan Kenobi Kanan Jarrus
IV
FORM IV
ATARU
The Way of the Hawk-Bat
▼
🟢 ACROBAT — SPEED AND FLIGHT
Ataru is pure energy. Jumping, flipping, spinning — use the Force to move your body in ways that seem impossible. Faster than the eye can follow. If you're fast enough, the enemy can never predict where your strike will come from.
⚡ KEY MOVE
The Horizon Strike — jump above the enemy, spin in the air, and come down with a diagonal strike from an angle they can't block.
🏋️ DRILLS TO PRACTISE
Jump and strike — jump off two feet, swing blade at the peak of the jump
360° spin with blade — full body rotation, blade in control the whole time
Fast combo: jump → land → immediate strike × 5 reps
Side roll drill — roll left, spring up, attack. Roll right, spring up, attack.
⚔️ MASTERS OF THIS FORM
Yoda Qui-Gon Jinn Ahsoka Tano
V
FORM V
DJEM SO
The Way of the Krayt Dragon
▼
🟠 POWER — COUNTER-ATTACK MASTER
Block hard. Hit back harder. Djem So takes every attack aimed at you and turns it into your counter-strike. The strength of the enemy becomes YOUR weapon. The harder they hit, the harder you hit back. Raw power and aggression — controlled.
⚡ KEY MOVE
The Power Block-Counter — meet the enemy's strike with full force, absorb it in your stance, then immediately drive forward with a powerful overhead strike.
🏋️ DRILLS TO PRACTISE
Strong stance — feet wide, knees bent, ready to take impact without moving
Hard parry drill — block with full arm strength, immediately counter × 10 reps
Power overhead strike × 20 — controlled but maximum force
Advance drill — one powerful step forward on every counter-attack
⚔️ MASTERS OF THIS FORM
Anakin Skywalker Luke Skywalker Darth Vader
VI
FORM VI
NIMAN
The Way of the Rancor
▼
🟣 DIPLOMAT — THE COMPLETE FORM
Niman combines pieces of all five forms before it — a little Shii-Cho, a little Makashi, some Soresu defence, Ataru speed, Djem So power — and adds the Force itself. A Niman user can push, pull and throw things at an enemy with the Force WHILE fighting with the blade. Hard to master, impossible to predict.
⚡ KEY MOVE
The Force Push Combo — use the Force to blast the enemy backward, close the distance in one leap, then finish with a blade combination before they recover.
🏋️ DRILLS TO PRACTISE
Mixed combo drill — use 2 moves from any two different forms in a row
Simulate Force Push — open-hand thrust, then immediately bring blade back up
Calm practice — slow, deliberate combinations without rushing any movement
Invent a 5-move combo using moves from 3 different forms
⚔️ MASTERS OF THIS FORM
Ki-Adi-Mundi Luminara Unduli Exar Kun
VII
FORM VII
JUYO / VAAPAD
The Way of the Vornskr
▼
🔴 MASTER LEVEL — EXTREME AGGRESSION
The most dangerous form ever created. Only the most disciplined Jedi can use it without falling to the dark side. You channel your own inner darkness — your fear, your anger, your fire — and use it as fuel. Ferocious, unpredictable, overwhelming. Mace Windu was the only Jedi to truly master Vaapad (the improved version) without losing himself.
⚡ KEY MOVE
The Vaapad Loop — your blade moves so fast it creates a web of attacks. Every strike flows into the next with zero pause. The opponent can't find a gap because there are no gaps.
🏋️ DRILLS TO PRACTISE
Speed burst drill — 10 strikes as fast as possible, full control, no pausing
Pressure advance — one strike every step forward, never step back
Continuous blade movement — keep blade flowing for 60 seconds without stopping
Mental control check — stop mid-drill, take one slow breath, then continue. Stay calm in the storm.
⚠️
WARNING: You must fully master Forms I–VI before attempting Juyo. Even Jedi Masters were forbidden from teaching this form. Train the others first.
⚔️ MASTERS OF THIS FORM
Mace Windu Darth Sidious Darth Maul

5 MINUTES · NEVER SKIP THIS
10 arm circles each direction
1 min — loosens your shoulders
20 jumping jacks
1 min — wakes the body up
Wrist rotations × 20 each hand
1 min — protects your grip
Check your space — no obstacles
30 sec — Jedi Rule #1
⚔️
PHASE 2 — FORM DRILLS
10 MINUTES · THE HEART OF TRAINING
Shii-Cho: 7-zone strikes × 3 rounds
2 min — always start with Form I
Current Form: 3 key drills from your chosen form
5 min — go to the 7 Forms tab for your drills
Footwork — 20 forward/back/left/right steps with blade up
3 min — feet win fights
💪
PHASE 3 — WARRIOR CONDITIONING
5 MINUTES · BUILD THE BODY
10 push-ups (or as many as you can)
Upper body strength for powerful strikes
20 squats
Strong legs = fast footwork = win
30-second plank
Core strength keeps your blade steady
🧘
PHASE 4 — COOL DOWN
3 MINUTES · A JEDI ALWAYS FINISHES CALM
3 deep slow breaths — in through nose, out through mouth
This is how you enter the Force
Arms + shoulder stretches × 30 sec each side
No injuries — you train again tomorrow
Power off saber + store safely
Respect the weapon. Always.

The 14-Day Warrior Method
Uncle Adam's Doctrine for Lucas Reinhard · Holiday 2026
📐 Program Design
  • 4 sessions/day — Learn (S1) · Drill (S2) · Bag (S3) · Game (S4)
  • 15–20 min each — a 6-year-old has 20 minutes of peak focus. Use it all, waste none.
  • 2h gaps minimum — recovery IS training. The muscles rebuild between sessions.
  • ONE focus per session — deep over wide. Mastery, not exposure.
  • Fitness Beast Days: Day 5 & Day 12 — no bag, pure conditioning. Animal circuits, sprints, star jumps, planks. Build the engine.
🥊 Technique Doctrine (Adam's Method)
  • HIGH JABS — arm fully extended ABOVE the head. Left × 30, right × 30. Non-negotiable daily drill.
  • Legs First — teep is the primary weapon. One push kick = 2 metres of distance. Punches are secondary.
  • The Warrior Spin — push from behind → STOMP → spin → hands up → STOP → teep. 5 steps. Automatic.
  • Combos — 1=jab, 2=cross, 3=hook. 1-2 · 1-1-2 · 1-2-3. Build in that order.
  • Footwork — shuffle, pivot, L-step. Distance = safety. Never cross the feet.
🛡️ Why We Train
We train to be kind. We train to respect people. We bow before and after.

But if someone attacks us — we protect with everything. Every. Single. Time.

We don't train because we WANT to fight. We train so we never HAVE to.
💡 For Lucas
You got pushed down. Every day. All year.
You got back up. Without crying. Without quitting.

That's already a warrior.

This training didn't MAKE you one.
It just gave your warrior a weapon.

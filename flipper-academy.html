FREQUENCY BASICS
Know what you're targeting before you point
FREQUENCYUSED FOR
433 MHzGarage doors, car remotes, alarms (most common in AU)
315 MHzOlder US car key fobs
868 MHzEuropean home automation, smart meters

Your garage remote is almost certainly 433 MHz. Most Australian RF devices are.

CAPTURE + SAVE
Record the signal permanently
Sub-GHz → Read RAW → Start → Press remote button → Stop → Save → name it

2

3
